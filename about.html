Our History

TechCon began in 2010 as a small meetup of software developers passionate about innovation and emerging technologies. Over the years, it has grown into one of the most anticipated technology conferences in the region, drawing professionals, entrepreneurs, and students from all around the world.

From early discussions on open-source collaboration to today’s deep dives into AI, cybersecurity, and green tech, TechCon has continuously evolved to reflect the ever-changing technology landscape.

Our Mission

TechCon’s mission is to inspire innovation, foster collaboration, and shape the future of technology through meaningful dialogue, hands-on workshops, and networking opportunities. We aim to empower individuals and organizations to harness technology for positive global impact.

Whether you’re a startup founder, a seasoned engineer, or a student with big ideas, TechCon is the place to share knowledge and spark the next wave of tech breakthroughs.

Notable Past Speakers

Over the years, TechCon has hosted some of the brightest minds and leaders in the tech industry. Here are a few of our inspiring past speakers:

Dr. Amina Patel

Dr. Patel is a leading researcher in artificial intelligence and ethics. Her 2022 keynote on “Responsible AI for a Sustainable Future” inspired global discussions on inclusive innovation.

Carlos Mendez

A renowned cybersecurity strategist, Carlos Mendez’s talk in 2021 shed light on data privacy and the future of digital trust in an interconnected world.

Lina Okafor

Founder of EcoTech Solutions, Lina Okafor spoke in 2023 about building sustainable tech startups that balance profit with environmental responsibility.
